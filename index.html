Who We Are

Housing is more than a roof.

At Anchor of Grace Havens, we believe home is where healing begins, dignity is restored, and hope has room to grow.

Founded by a U.S. Navy veteran, our company is being built to serve veterans with warmth, professionalism, accountability, and genuine care. Our focus is shared housing that feels safe, respectful, and welcoming.

Veteran Housing

A welcoming shared-living model.

Our housing model is designed for veterans who need a stable home environment while working toward their next chapter. We are developing partnerships with veteran-serving organizations, housing programs, landlords, and community providers.

  • Furnished shared housing
  • Clean and respectful common spaces
  • Structured house expectations
  • Community and program partnerships
  • A focus on safety, dignity, and stability

Who We Serve

Veterans seeking a fresh start

Our long-term vision includes housing opportunities for women veterans, single-parent veterans, veterans recovering from housing instability, and other veterans referred through qualified programs and partners.

Housing availability and eligibility will depend on property readiness, referral sources, program requirements, and applicable laws.

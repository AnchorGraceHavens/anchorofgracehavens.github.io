Who We Are

Housing is more than a roof.

Home is where healing begins, dignity is restored, and hope has room to grow.

Anchor of Grace Havens LLC is a veteran-founded housing company created to provide safe, respectful, and professionally managed shared-living environments for veterans. We are building a bridge between military service and civilian stability through housing, accountability, community partnerships, and compassionate support.

Our Promise

Every veteran who walks through our doors will be treated with dignity, respect, warmth, and the belief that a fresh start is possible.

Veteran Housing

A shared-living model built around safety, structure, and respect.

Our homes are designed for veterans who need a stable place to reset and move forward. The model emphasizes cleanliness, personal responsibility, peaceful shared spaces, and coordination with qualified referral and support partners.

  • Furnished shared housing
  • Professional house expectations
  • Safe and respectful common areas
  • Program and community referrals
  • Focus on long-term independence

Housing availability and eligibility depend on property readiness, referral sources, program requirements, and applicable laws.

Meet the Founder

Tabitha Davis

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Tabitha Davis is a U.S. Navy veteran and the founder of Anchor of Grace Havens LLC. Her vision is rooted in service, faith, compassion, and the belief that veterans deserve more than temporary shelter—they deserve a stable home environment where dignity can be restored and hope can grow.

She created Anchor of Grace Havens to help build practical pathways from housing instability toward employment, independence, healing, and long-term success.

“We are not simply providing housing. We are creating room for veterans to breathe, rebuild, and believe in their future again.”
